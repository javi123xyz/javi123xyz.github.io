Known Site Issues


▸The main issues on this site revolve around compatability with mobile devices. I tried to make the design of the portfolio as responsive as I could to provide a uniform experience to viewers on all platforms and devices
▸This is also an issue primarily for mobile browsers. I use the :hover selector fairly often throughout the site, but it's exclusively for aesthetic purpopses and will not limit accessibility (but if it does, please let me know).
▸I also tried using CSS that is comaptible with all major browsers. I have tested most of the features and it seems that some of the filters may not properly work on firefox. Hopefully compatability with browsers isn't too bad, but I do recommend using the latest version of Google Chrome to view my portfolio, as that is the browser it was primarily developed and tested on.
▸Other browser compatability issues will probably arise with displaying PDFs on the Academic works page and also the 3D models. I will try and look for solutions if these issues persist.
▸The other top issue right now is SPEED. I'm mostly talking about the speed at which media such as audio and images are loaded and displayed. One of the reasons why the site has this issue is because of its hosting, or should I say, the lack thereof. Being the shrewd, broke college student that I am, I found a way to host my own HTML and files online for free through Google Drive. Though it met my needs, it has obvious limitations.
