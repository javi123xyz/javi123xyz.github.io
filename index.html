Welcomes You To His Creative Portfolio


My name is Javier Solis. I enjoy making creative content of all sorts and throughout the years, I have accumulated a good amount of it. Though I have shared bits of pieces of it with friends and family, this content was not centralized in one place to be able to easily and efficiently share with a wider audience. So utilizing the bountiful body of knowledge that is the internet, I taught myself some web design (HTML5, CSS3, and some JavaScript) and built a personal portfolio entirely from scratch. I want this portfolio to be a space where I can show off my graphic design work.
(Note: While you can utilize your phone or tablet to view my website, I recommend you use a desktop or laptop instead to get the full experience. I am still working on making everything accessible and uniform throughout all devices and platforms, so please email me any issues you encounter so I can work on them!)
